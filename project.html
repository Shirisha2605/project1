Advantages and Disadvatages

Having a comfortable environment to study can be crucial to many students. While some prefer to study in libraries, many find that their homes are a much more suitable location. Some students dont enjoy traditional classrooms and having to speak in front of many people. This is why sometimes, being able to follow the coursework online will make the information more digestible to them. Having a comfortable environment to learn, in most cases, helps students to be more motivated and achieve better results.
